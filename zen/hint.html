Hints and Tips

  • Sometimes it's better to simply avoid monsters rather than use up arrows killing them.
  • You can kill monsters by dropping objects on top of them which can really help to save arrows!
  • Try to build up shielding early on as this will help you survive later in the game.
  • The best vending machines sell 4 life-points per Zenq so try to remember where they are!
  • With a few exceptions, keys will unlock doors in the general vicinity.
